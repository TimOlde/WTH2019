How to use Volo?

Let's hear your voice

Don't know what to watch tonight? Don't worry! Just ask Volo to recommend you something. Volo understands of voice commands, and you can ask it to play music, movies, TV, and so much more.

A fully customisable experience

Go to the Volo Market to check out the apps developed for Volo, to get even more out of it. Here, there are apps in categories ranging from entertainment/games to social media.

Volo in YeloPlay

Volo can connect to Yelo Play to know your preferences, and accept voice commands from your phone anywhere.
